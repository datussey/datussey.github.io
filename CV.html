Experience


Research assistant— University of Florida, Gainesville, FL— 2017 - present
Thesis research: Improving field performance of sterilized Aedes aegypti mosquitoes for use in sterile insect technique

Research assistant— University of Minnesota, St. Paul, MN— 2015 - 2017
Thesis research: Investigating the sublethal effects of cold on emerald ash borer (Coleoptera: Buprestidae), focusing on nutrition levels and dispersal capabilities

Research Assistant— Mountain Horticultural Crops Research and Extension Center, Mills River, NC — 2013 - 2015
My work included plot set up and maintenance of vegetable fields and tree fruit orchards, monitoring crops for insect damage, and applying insecticidesticides in test plots. I also coordinated with various commercial growers, testing alternate application methods of insecticides. This extension work included creating informational handouts on agricultural pests and insecticide application methods. Conducted annual surveys across North Carolina for presence of Halyomorpha halys.

Summer Scholar— New York State Agricultural Experiment Station, Geneva, NY— 2012
My principal research duties involved testing the attractiveness of multiple synthetic sex pheromones for Prionus laticollis for use in IPM strategies. I also provided support for the setup, maintenance, and monitoring of insect traps for orchard IPM control programs against pests such as plum curculio and coddling moth.

Outreach
